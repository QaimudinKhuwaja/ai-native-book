# Challenges and Future of AI-Native

While AI-Native Driven Development offers immense potential, it also presents a unique set of challenges that require careful consideration and innovative solutions.

## Key Challenges

- **Complexity**: Managing highly distributed systems with integrated AI components can be complex.
- **Talent Gap**: Shortage of professionals with expertise across AI, DevOps, and Data Engineering.
- **Cost**: AI workloads can be resource-intensive, leading to high infrastructure costs.
- **Ethical AI**: Ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability in AI systems.
- **Security**: Protecting sensitive data and AI models from adversarial attacks.
- **Interoperability**: Integrating diverse AI models and frameworks.

## The Future Landscape

The future of AI-Native is bright, with continuous advancements in:
- **Foundation Models**: Leveraging large pre-trained models for various AI tasks.
- **Automated MLOps**: Further automation of ML pipelines, reducing manual effort.
- **Edge AI**: Deploying AI models closer to data sources for real-time inference and reduced latency.
- **Responsible AI**: Developing and deploying AI systems that are fair, transparent, and secure.
- **AI-Native Cloud Platforms**: Cloud providers offering services specifically designed for AI-Native development.
